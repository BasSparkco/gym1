# Later Roadmap

This document captures features and ideas that should stay out of MVP messaging.
They remain important, but they should be positioned as later-phase capabilities.

## Move Out of MVP

The following items should not appear as MVP commitments unless clearly marked as later:

* Face recognition
* Fingerprint integration
* RFID cards
* Turnstile integration
* Payroll
* POS
* Accounting
* Nutrition plans
* Training plans
* Mobile applications
* AI features

## Later-Phase Feature Groups

### Advanced Access Control

* Face recognition devices
* Fingerprint systems
* RFID and NFC access
* Turnstiles and smart gate integrations
* Anti-passback rules
* Visitor access passes
* Device health monitoring

### Commerce and Back Office

* POS for front desk and retail sales
* Barcode and receipt printing
* Product inventory and stock movement
* Purchase orders and supplier management
* Expense tracking
* Cash management
* Revenue analytics
* Accounting integrations

### Fitness Services

* Training programs
* Exercise library
* Progress tracking
* Nutrition plans
* Meal scheduling
* Coach notes
* Body measurements and assessment dashboards

### Employee and Operations Expansion

* Payroll
* Shift planning
* Task management
* Internal approvals
* Commission tracking for trainers and sales staff

### Mobile Experience

* Member mobile app
* Employee mobile app
* Digital membership card
* Push notifications
* Mobile class booking
* In-app payments

### AI and Automation

* Churn prediction
* Member engagement scoring
* Attendance forecasting
* Smart renewal reminders
* AI nutrition recommendations
* Personalized workout suggestions
* AI support assistant

## Future Product Ideas

* White-label support for enterprise customers
* Franchise management for multi-brand operators
* Corporate memberships and B2B plans
* Family accounts and dependent memberships
* Referral and loyalty programs
* Promotions and coupon engine
* Public API for partners
* Marketplace for coaches, classes, and add-ons
* CRM and marketing automation
* Lead capture and sales pipeline management
* Waivers, contracts, and digital signatures
* Online joining and self-service onboarding
* Regional payment gateway integrations
* VAT and tax configuration by country
* Multi-currency pricing
* Advanced BI dashboards
* Data warehouse and analytics exports

## Recommendation

Keep MVP focused on the operational core:

* Tenant and branch setup
* Authentication and permissions
* Member management
* Membership plans and renewals
* Visit tracking
* QR or manual access
* Notifications
* Membership payments
* Core reports

Everything else should be described as a later phase to reduce delivery risk and keep the first release realistic.
