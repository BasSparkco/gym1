Prepared By: SparkCo

# Executive Summary

The goal of this project is to build a modern cloud-based Gym Management Platform for gyms, fitness centers, and multi-branch fitness operators, with future expansion into martial arts academies, swimming clubs, and broader sports organizations.

The platform will be designed as a Software as a Service (SaaS) solution, allowing clubs to subscribe and use the system through the web without the need for local installation. Mobile applications remain part of the long-term roadmap rather than the initial MVP.

The long-term vision is to establish a scalable platform that can serve customers in Palestine, Israel, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, and other regional markets.

---

# Project Vision

To create a modern, reliable, and scalable sports club management platform that combines:

* Club Management
* Membership Management
* Access Control
* Communication
* Reporting

within a single integrated ecosystem.

The initial product focus is a practical MVP for operational gym workflows. Advanced back-office, mobile, hardware, and AI capabilities will be delivered in later phases.

---

# Why This Project?

Current solutions in the market often suffer from:

* Outdated user interfaces
* Limited mobile support
* Weak integration capabilities
* Slow feature development
* Poor customer experience

The proposed platform aims to address these challenges through modern technologies and continuous development.

---

# Business Model

The project will be developed as a SaaS platform.

Instead of selling software licenses once, the platform will operate using recurring subscriptions.

Benefits:

* Continuous revenue stream
* Continuous product improvement
* Ongoing customer support
* Easier customer onboarding
* Long-term business sustainability
